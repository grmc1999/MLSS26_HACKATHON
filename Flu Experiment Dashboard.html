Experiment Summary

This experiment used a conditional diffusion model (DiffusionForecaster + ConditionalDenoiser) for cross-country ILI forecasting, trained on US CDC data and tested on WHO FluID data (France, Mexico, Australia, South Africa). The pipeline ran 37 iterations with RAG-guided modifications to env/train.py.

Best result: Test MAE 16.57 (92.5% improvement from baseline 221.05).
