Experiment Summary

This experiment used a conditional diffusion model (DiffusionForecaster + ConditionalDenoiser) for cross-country ILI forecasting, trained on US CDC data and tested on WHO FluID data (France, Mexico, Australia, South Africa). The pipeline ran 5 iterations with RAG-guided modifications to env/train.py.

Best result: Test MAE 19.01 (92.0% improvement from baseline 238.77).
